# Product Vision

> Related: [PRD](PRD.md) | [Future Ideas](future-ideas.md) | [Architecture](../architecture/overview.md)

## Core idea

ZoomAlboom is an Android-first interactive multimedia album where users create spatial stories on an infinite zoomable canvas.

The album is not a sequence of pages.
It is a navigable space that users can move through.

## Content types

Users can place:
- photos
- videos
- text
- stickers
- vector shapes
- audio (future)

## Core interactions

Users can:
- pan
- zoom
- rotate
- move between frames
- focus on a frame (animated camera transition)

## Product metaphor

ZoomAlboom is similar in spirit to ZoomNotes / Prezi, but focused on media storytelling rather than presentations. The goal is a "Prezi about life" — an explorable spatial story, not a static gallery.

---

## Main use cases

### Family album
Photos, captions, stories, videos, relatives, and generational structures.
The canvas allows both a family overview and deep personal stories inside one spatial world.

### Travel diary
Frames as cities/places, with media, notes, and maps.
A route map widget and city/day frames let you zoom from overview to individual memories.

### Cookbook
Recipes, step-by-step photos, and short videos.
Recipe index, ingredient views, and seasonal groupings in a visual food world.

### Educational album
Illustrations, explanations, diagrams, visual stories.
Concept maps, checklists, and before/after comparisons as canvas-native objects.

### Project album
Documentation, process, references, and results arranged spatially.
Timeline, milestones, and media evidence organized in a navigable project space.

### School / Kindergarten album
A school-years or preschool album organized by class, teachers, classmates, events, and milestones.

May include:
- school years / kindergarten years by age or class
- classmates and teachers
- performances, holidays, field trips
- drawings and crafts
- certificates and achievements
- yearly progression photos
- funny quotes and memories

Useful widgets: School Years / Timeline, Classmates, Teachers, Calendar, Drawings Gallery, Achievements, Highlights, People.

### Baby / Child Growth album
A child-focused album organized by age, developmental milestones, places, people, and media.

May include:
- birth and early months
- developmental milestones (first smile, first steps, first words)
- yearly progression
- family, relatives, friends
- places visited
- hobbies and interests as the child grows

Useful widgets: Growth Timeline, Milestones, Calendar, Highlights, People.

### Genealogy / Family History album
A family-history album organized around people, branches, places, old photos, documents, and stories.

May include:
- family tree
- individual person pages
- family branches and generations
- ancestral homes and places
- historical documents and old photographs
- family stories and oral history

Useful widgets: Family Tree, People, Place Map, Timeline.

### Hobby album
A long-term hobby or creative practice documented spatially.

May include:
- gallery of works
- process photos and videos
- achievements and milestones
- related people
- events, exhibitions, competitions
- timeline of progress

### Family Archive / Life Story album
A broader "life story" album combining diary entries, media, people, places, time, and AI-generated summaries. Designed as a living document that grows over time with AI Diary integration.
